Why It Matters

Important Information Is Scattered — Until It Isn’t

Most families have financial accounts, insurance policies, retirement plans, legal documents, passwords, and final wishes spread across filing cabinets, email inboxes, online portals, and personal notes. It feels manageable day to day — until something unexpected happens.

When a loved one passes away or becomes incapacitated, family members are suddenly tasked with finding critical information under enormous emotional stress. Accounts go unclaimed. Policies are never located. Wishes go unknown. The burden falls on those who are already grieving.

The Prosperity Life & Legacy Organizer brings everything together in one place — so your family can focus on what truly matters, not on searching for paperwork.

📋

One Place for Everything

All of your important accounts, policies, documents, and personal information organized in a single, easy-to-use resource that your family can access when they need it most.

💚

Peace of Mind

Knowing that your loved ones won’t have to search through boxes or guess at account details gives you real peace of mind — and gives your family a gift they will truly appreciate.

🔒

Clarity and Continuity

A well-organized legacy plan reduces confusion, prevents conflict, and provides continuity during difficult times — ensuring your wishes are known and your affairs can be handled smoothly.

What’s Inside the Organizer

The Life & Legacy Organizer covers every important category your family may need.

👤

Personal Information

Full legal name, date of birth, Social Security number, identification documents, and contact details.

📞

Emergency Contacts

Key people your family should notify, including doctors, attorneys, accountants, and financial advisors.

🏦

Financial Accounts

Bank accounts, checking, savings, investment accounts, and brokerage information with institutions and account numbers.

📈

Retirement Plans

401(k), 403(b), TSP, IRA, pension, and annuity information including beneficiary designations and contact details.

🛡️

Insurance Policies

Life insurance, health, Medicare, long-term care, and final expense policies with policy numbers and carrier contacts.

🏠

Property & Assets

Real estate, vehicles, valuables, and other significant assets with locations, titles, and estimated values.

🔑

Passwords & Digital Accounts

Online account login information, email, social media, streaming services, and digital subscriptions to manage or close.

⚖️

Legal Documents

Will, trust, power of attorney, healthcare directive, and other important legal documents with storage locations.

🕊️

Final Wishes

Burial or cremation preferences, memorial service wishes, obituary notes, and personal messages for loved ones.

📝

Instructions for Family

Step-by-step guidance for your family on what to do, who to call, and what decisions need to be made.
