We may collect the following types of personal information when you voluntarily provide it through forms on our website:

  • Contact information: your name, email address, phone number

We use the information you provide to:

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

When you complete a form on our website — including consultation request forms, guide download forms, or the contact form — the information you submit is sent directly to Prosperity Life & Financial Solutions LLC for follow-up. By providing your phone number and checking the consent box on any form on this website, you expressly consent to receive SMS text messages from Prosperity Life & Financial Solutions LLC. These messages may include:

We do not share your phone number or SMS consent with third parties for their own marketing purposes. Mobile opt-in data and consent are never shared with third parties.
